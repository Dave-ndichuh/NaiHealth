    Cardiology

    Leading heart care, from diagnosis to surgery and rehabilitation.

    Our cardiology department specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing heart-related conditions, offering advanced cardiac care including surgery, stress tests, and more.

    Neurology

    Expert care for neurological disorders and brain health.

    Our neurology department offers cutting-edge diagnostic tools and treatment options for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system.

    Hepatology

    Specialized care for liver diseases and conditions.

    Our hepatology team offers expertise in diagnosing and treating liver disorders, including cirrhosis, hepatitis, and fatty liver disease.

    Our pediatric department provides expert care for children of all ages, from newborns to teenagers, ensuring their growth, development, and health are closely monitored.

    Ophthalmology

    Comprehensive eye care, from routine check-ups to advanced treatments.

    Our ophthalmology department provides a range of services from eye exams and corrective procedures to the treatment of eye diseases and conditions like cataracts and glaucoma.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to the most common questions about our healthcare services.

    How do I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Now" button on our website or by calling our customer service at +254 700 000 000.

    What are the available payment options?

    We accept payment via cash, mobile money (M-Pesa), and credit card. You can also opt for our affordable health insurance plans.

    What should I bring to my first consultation?

    For your first visit, please bring your ID card, insurance details (if applicable), and any previous medical records or prescriptions.

    Do you offer emergency services?

    Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care. Please call our emergency hotline at +254 700 000 111 in case of an emergency.

    Can I receive medical services without insurance?

    Yes, we offer medical services to all patients, regardless of insurance status. We provide affordable payment plans to suit your needs.

    Do you offer online consultations?

    Yes, we offer online consultations via video calls. Simply book your appointment, and choose the option for an online consultation.
